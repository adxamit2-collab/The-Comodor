Our Story

A Taste of Italy, Right Here in Surrey

For over 25 years, The Comodor has been a beloved fixture in Byfleet, Surrey. Our family-run restaurant was born from a passion for authentic Italian cooking — recipes passed down through generations, prepared with the finest seasonal ingredients.

From wood-fired pizzas to hand-rolled pasta and premium cuts of steak, every dish is crafted with love and attention to detail. We believe great food brings people together, and every meal here is a celebration.
